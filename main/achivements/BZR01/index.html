Supra SAEINDIA 2022

Day 1

We began with registrations and pit setup, followed by safety scrutiny. In the first round of technical inspection (TI), judges provided feedback on our car, prompting focused efforts from us.

Day 2

Efficient progress in static events: CAE, Business Plan, Design Presentation, Cost Report. Car passed 1st TI round, excelled in Weight, Egress, Tilt tests, concluding the day's achievements.

Day 3

BZR-02 excelled in Noise and Brake tests during TI. Missed Acceleration, Autocross, Skid pad due to time. Anticipation for the upcoming race day remains high.

DAY 4

The event most looked forward to was here, the Endurance Event.
The 10 cars, including BZR-02, assembled and the race began, our car shone brightly, roaring as it sped before everyone, filling us with pride.
Unfortunately towards the 3rd lap's conclusion, BZR-02 eased and eventually halted.
Despite our disappointment, we appreciated seeing our car on the track.
